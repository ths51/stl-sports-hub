Blues Acquire Kevin Hayes From Philadelphia

Philly retains half of Hayes' salary, Blues give up 2024 6th round pick

The Blues have traded a 6th Round Pick in the 2024 NHL Entry Draft to the Philadelphia Flyers in exchange for forward Kevin Hayes. In addition, the Flyers have agreed to retain half of Hayes' salary. He has about $7.15 million remaining on his contract for each of the next three seasons.

Last season, Hayes put up a career high in points (54) despite reports that his coach didn't like him or his style of play and that he played on a bad team.
